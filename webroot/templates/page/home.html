Use the same password everywhere. Securely.

Password Ninja creates a unique password for every website you use based on your master password and the website's domain

Don't trust anyone else with your password. Even us.

Just like a bank pin, you should never trust your password to anyone else. All it takes is for one insecure website you use to get hacked and someone else has your password. And let's face it - you've used it everywhere.

Password Ninja creates a unique password for every website you use, but you only need to remember one of them. Password Ninja uses one-way encryption to create a unique password based on your master password and the website you are using. In non-tech speak, we take your password and the website's name, put them together and use this to create a unique password. So from your master password and the website you can easily "calculate" your unique password for the website. But you can't go backwards to find out your master password from any website password.
